About Us

Building Paths Initiative is an illustrious organization that fervently champions the cause of education, ensuring that it becomes a reality for all. With unwavering commitment, we extend our support to adolescents through a harmonious blend of scholarships and comprehensive educational materials , paving the way for a seamless and transformative journey towards knowledge and a bright future.

But our commitment doesn't stop there. We recognize the vital importance of addressing the unique needs of adolescents, including their menstrual health. We endeavour to alleviate the burdens faced by students, ensuring their well-being by providing essential sanitary pads to those in need. We strive to empower these young minds to excel in their academic pursuits without the barrier imposed by menstrual challenges.

Moreover, Building Paths Initiative recognizes the significance of comprehensive sex education and body safety sensitization for school students. With a holistic approach, we aim to equip these young individuals with the knowledge, understanding, and tools they need to navigate their personal boundaries and foster healthy relationships. Through our pioneering efforts, we empower students to make informed choices, nuturing a culture of respect and well-being.

At Building Paths Initiative, we believe that education is the cornerstone of a flourishing society, and we dedicate ourselves wholeheartedly to ensuring that no student is left behind.

Our Vision

We envision a barrier-free world of education while promoting menstrual health and comprehensive sex education for all.

Our Mission

To empower adolescents through education, scholarships, and support while addressing their needs in areas like menstrual health and comprehensive sex education. And we are on a mission to ensure that no Child is left behind.
